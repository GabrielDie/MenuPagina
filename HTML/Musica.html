ABOUT THE MUSIC

This musical genre is called black metal, is an American musical group formed in 1989. It generated great scandal for its time, because the metal genre was related to the dark arts or demons, because of their way of singing.

ABOUT THE ARTIST

Bengt Mikael Stanne born 20 May 1974 is the vocalist and one of the lyricists as well as a former rhythm guitarist, for the Swedish melodic death metal band Dark Tranquillity. Since 2021, Stanne is the only remaining original member of the band. Stanne is also the vocalist for death metal band Grand Cadaver, which formed in 2020. He was also an ex-vocalist for Swedish bands HammerFall and In Flames.
